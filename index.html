African Dev Continental Summit

Welcome's You To Makurdi

2020

The African Dev(AfDEV) Continental Summit is a biannual conference held in Makurdi, Benue State, where tech giants interact with business tycoons to profer IT solutions to various economic challenges in Africa. This concept was pioneered by Engr Aye Daniel Asoo (MNSE, COREN).

20.12.2020(TUE)~21(WED)

@Aminu Isa Kontagora Theatre, Makurdi Old Otukpo Road Benue State.
